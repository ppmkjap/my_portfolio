About me

From Operations to Salesforce —
a career built on curiosity

I started my career in Operations, where I developed a deep love for process, structure, strategizing and execution. But I wanted more — I wanted to solve problems, not just follow procedures. When a career coach introduced me to Salesforce, it was a perfect fit to my persona, and I loved the structure it offered while still allowing me to exercise my creativity in problem solving and troubleshooting.

Within 3 months I had my first Admin certification. That decision changed my life. Over the next 12 years — including eight at Workday — I grew from a case handler into a Senior Business Systems Analyst, owning programs that touched every corner of the organization. From being an everyday BSA, I became the subject matter expert within my group for SFDC Security/Access, Enterprise Territory Management/Sharing Models, New Offering Launches, SOX Compliance, and other Workday specific programs.

"I became the go-to person for my entire team — not because I was assigned the role, but because I earned it over 8 years by never saying no and never leaving a problem half-solved. My manager recognized this early and formally appointed me as the mentor for every new hire — a role I took seriously every single time."

Today, I bring that same ownership mindset, documentation discipline, detail orientation, and cross-functional instinct to every team I work with. I also actively use AI tools including Claude and Gemini to accelerate my work. I enjoy learning new tools and technologies to continually push the boundaries of my knowledge and accelerate execution at my work.
